fn

Object static

Defined in src/www/components/lavaca/src/util/DateUtils.js:136

Object containing the functions used by each date format code. Default format codes are:

d
Day of month (1 - 31)
dd
Padded day of month (01 - 31)
ddd
Abbreviated day of week (Sun - Sat)
ddd
Full day of week (Sunday - Saturday)
f
Tenth of a second
ff
Hundreth of a second
fff
Milliseconds
h
Twelve-hour clock hour (1 - 12)
hh
Padded twelve-hour clock hour (01 - 12)
H
Twenty-four hour clock hour (0 - 23)
HH
Padded twenty-four hour clock hour (00 - 23)
m
Minute (0 - 59)
mm
Padded minute (00 - 59)
M
Month (1 - 12)
MM
Padded month (01 - 12)
MMM
Abbreviated month (Jan - Dec)
MMMM
Full month (January - December)
s
Second (0 - 59)
ss
Padded second (00 - 59)
t
Abbreviated AM/PM designator (A or P)
tt
Full AM/PM designator (AM or PM)
y
Short year (0 - 99)
yy
Padded short year (00 - 99)
yyy
Full year padded to at least 3 digits (000+)
yyyy
Full year padded to at least 4 digits (0000+)
z
Hours offset from UTC (-12, 0, 12)
zz
Padded hours offset from UTC (-12, 00, 12)
zzz
Padded hours and minute offset from UTC (-12:00, 00:00, 12:00)

To add a custom format code, assign this property an object containing an i function (responsible for parsing) and an o function (responsible for stringifying). The i function should assign to one of the following properties of its second argument: date, month, year, hour, minute, second, ms, or offset. Example: Lavaca.util.DateUtils.fn.QQQ = {i: function(input, dateObj, mappedObj) { dateObj.date = parseInt(input, 10); }, o: function(date, utc) { return (utc ? date.getUTCDate() : date.getDate()).toString(); }};
